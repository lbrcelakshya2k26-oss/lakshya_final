About LAKSHYA

LAKSHYA is the premier National-Level Technical & Cultural Fest of LBRCE (Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering). It is a melting pot of innovation, technology, creativity, and vibrant culture packed into one explosive day.

LAKSHYA serves as a high-energy platform where young minds compete in technical hackathons during the day and showcase mesmerizing cultural performances by night.
