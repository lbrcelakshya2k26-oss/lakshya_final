ABOUT LAKSHYA

LBRCE’s National Level Techno–Cultural Fest

LBRCE, Mylavaram

The Flagship Fest

LAKSHYA is the National Level Technical & Cultural Fest of Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering (LBRCE), Mylavaram. As the college’s flagship annual event, it serves as a vibrant platform that brings together innovation, creativity, and celebration.

Organized by the students of LBRCE with strong academic and institutional support, LAKSHYA offers a diverse range of technical competitions, cultural showcases, and special events. The fest provides a unique opportunity for students to explore emerging technologies, present innovative ideas, and demonstrate their creative talents on a grand stage.

With participation from students across various engineering institutions, LAKSHYA aims to encourage learning beyond classrooms, collaboration, leadership, and holistic development. Over the years, it has grown into a recognized national-level fest that truly reflects the energetic spirit and technical excellence of LBRCE.
