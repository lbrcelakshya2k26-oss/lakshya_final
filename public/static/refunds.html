Refund & Cancellation Policy

Note: Registration fees once paid are generally non-refundable. Please review the specific conditions below.

1. General Cancellation Policy

Participants may cancel their registration at any time; however, no refunds will be provided for voluntary cancellations by the participant. Please ensure you are available for the event dates before registering.

2. Duplicate Payments (Technical Error)

If you have been charged twice for the same event due to a technical error (e.g., server timeout or network issue), the extra amount will be automatically refunded to the original payment source. This process is initiated automatically and typically takes 5-7 business days to reflect in your bank account.

3. Event Cancellation by Organizers

In the unlikely event that a specific competition or workshop is cancelled by the LAKSHYA 2K26 Committee and not rescheduled, a full refund of the registration fee for that specific event will be processed within 7-10 working days.

4. Processing Timelines

All valid refunds are processed via our payment gateway partner, Razorpay. Once initiated by us, the credit to your bank account/card depends on your bank's processing time (usually 5-7 business days).

5. Contact for Disputes

For any payment-related issues, please email us at support@testify-lac.com with the subject line "Payment Dispute - [Your Transaction ID]".
