Shipping & Delivery Policy

This Shipping & Delivery Policy outlines how LAKSHYA 2K26 handles delivery of services after payment. Since our platform sells **digital event registrations and passes**, no physical shipment is involved.

1. Nature of Services

LAKSHYA 2K26 offers registrations for cultural, technical and sports events. All services are **completely digital** and delivered electronically.

2. Delivery of Confirmation

Once the payment is successfully completed via Razorpay:

  • A confirmation email is sent to your registered email ID.
  • Your registration details will appear instantly under the **My Registrations** section.
  • A digital receipt is emailed automatically.

3. Delivery Time

Delivery is **instant**. You should receive your confirmation email within **1–5 minutes** of payment.

4. Non-Receipt of Confirmation Email

If you do not receive your confirmation email:

  • Check your spam/junk folder.
  • Verify your email address in your profile.
  • Contact our support team at support@testify-lac.com.

5. Incorrect Email / Mobile Number

If a user enters an incorrect email or mobile number during registration, LAKSHYA 2K26 is not responsible for failed delivery. However, you may contact support for correction.

6. No Physical Shipping

LAKSHYA 2K26 does not ship any physical goods. All deliverables (registration ID, confirmation mail, receipts) are provided digitally only.

7. Event Entry

After successful payment, your registration ID is used for verification at the event venue. You will not receive any courier/postage-based material.

8. Contact Us

For any issues regarding delivery or confirmation, please contact:

Email: support@testify-lac.com
